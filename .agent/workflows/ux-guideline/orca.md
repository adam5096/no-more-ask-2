description: ORCA - 甚麼是 ORCA
---

is a iterative OOUX framework that helps translate research andrequirements into clarified fesign structure - and requires blue, yellow, pink, and greend sticky notes!
O: Objects, blue notes
R: Relationships, blue notes
C: CTA (call to action), green notes
A: Attributes, mixed color, red and yellow notes

We the OOUXers, answer these questions before designing "a single screen":
* What are the OBJECTS in the user's mental model ?
* What are the objects' RELATIONSHIPS to each other?
* What CALLS-TO-ACTION do objects offer users?
* What are the ATTRIBUTES that mak up the objects?

Round 1: Discover (ask amazing questions) 
Object Discorvery, Relationship Discorvery, CTA Discorvery, Attributes Discorvery;

Round 2: Requirements (practice UX at high-resolution)
Object Requirements , Relationship Requirements, CTA Requirements, Attributes Requirements;

Round 3: Prioritization (iterate strategically)
Object Prioritization , Relationship Prioritization, CTA Prioritization, Attributes Prioritization;

Round 4: Representation (design naturally intuitive screens and components)
* step 1: Sketching,
* step 2: Prototyping
* step 3: Testing

Quick tips / a checklist for next action
O: ID the objects within your system;
R: Map the relationships;
C: ID roles, and list CTAs for each object
A: List attributes for each object
